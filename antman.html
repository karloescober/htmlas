Antman

Synopsis

Ant-Man is a superhero movie about a cat burglar named Scott Lang who is given a super-suit with the ability to shrink in scale but increase in strength. He must help his mentor, Dr. Hank Pym, protect the secret behind his Ant-Man suit from a new generation of towering threats
