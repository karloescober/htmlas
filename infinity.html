Avengers: Infinity War

Synopsis

In Avengers: Infinity War, the Avengers and the Guardians of the Galaxy attempt to prevent Thanos from collecting the six all-powerful Infinity Stones. Thanos plans to use the artifacts to inflict his twisted will on reality, and his goal is to kill half of all life in the universe. The Avengers unite to battle their most powerful enemy yet. The fate of Earth and existence has never been more uncertain.
